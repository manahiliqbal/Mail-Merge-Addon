How to Use Mail Merge

Welcome to the Mail Merge add-on guide! This guide will walk you through the steps of using the Mail Merge add-on to send personalized emails using data from your Google Sheets.

Overview

Mail Merge allows you to:

  • Select or upload email templates.
  • Personalize the email content using data from Google Sheets.
  • Preview the personalized email.
  • Schedule the emails to be sent at a specified time.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Preparing Your Google Sheet

Ensure your Google Sheet has the following columns:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Email Address
  • Any other data you want to use for personalization.
2. Selecting an Email Template

You have three options to select an email template:

Option A: Select a Pre-built Template
  1. In the Mail Merge interface, select Select a Pre-built Template from the dropdown.
  2. A new dropdown will appear labeled Select Template Document.
  3. Choose a template from the dropdown list.
Option B: Upload a Template from Local Storage
  1. In the Mail Merge interface, select Upload a Template from Local Storage from the dropdown.
  2. Click on the Upload Template File button.
  3. Select and upload your email template file from your local storage.
Option C: Write an Email
  1. In the Mail Merge interface, select Write an Email from the dropdown.
  2. Enter the email subject in the Email Subject field.
  3. Write your email content in the editor provided.
  4. Use {{FirstName}}, {{LastName}}, and other placeholders to personalize your email.
3. Previewing the Email
  1. Click the Preview Email button to see how the personalized email will look for one of the recipients.
  2. A modal will appear showing the preview of the email.
  3. Ensure the placeholders are correctly replaced with the actual data from your Google Sheet.
4. Scheduling the Emails
  1. Click the Schedule Email button.
  2. The scheduling page will open.
  3. Enter the scheduled email date in the Scheduled Email Date field.
  4. Select the time zone from the Select Time Zone dropdown.
  5. Enter the scheduled email time in the Scheduled Email Time field.
  6. Enter the time interval between each email in the Time interval between each email (in seconds) field.
  7. Click the Schedule Emails button to schedule the emails.
